About the project

The Center for Open Education “Middle of the Earth” was created in the Department of Humanities of the Baikal BRICS Institute, IRNITU with the aim of promoting the Russian language as a foreign language and the traditions of Russian higher education. The project is supported by the Ministry of Education of the Russian Federation.

Key tasks of the project:

  • popularization of Russian academic culture
  • linguistic and cultural adaptation of foreign students
  • acquaintance with the Baikal regions
  • presentation of educational and career opportunities in the Baikal region

Courses available

Road to Russia - Russian as a foreign language
Russian language for study and work in Russia: first level (exam TORFL-1/B1)
Easy
The course will tell you how to successfully pass the Level 1 Certificate exam, which indicates the average level of language competence that meets the basic communicative needs in the domestic, cultural, educational and professional fields. In addition, a first-level certificate allows its holder to enter a Russian university.
Conquest of Siberia (A2)
Hard
This course will tell you about how the Cossacks conquered Siberia. To improve the basic skills of the Russian language, acquaintance with the perfective and imperfective verbs denoting movement, as well as how prefixes can change the meaning of verbs, will help.
