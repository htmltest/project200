About the Project
Study and Work at Lake Baikal offers INRTU students from diverse backgrounds a unique chance to gain a top-tier education at a leading Russian technical university and jumpstart their careers in high-growth sectors of the Irkutsk region.
Irkutsk National Research Technical University is seeking talented students to participate in the Study and Work at Lake Baikal project, which is being implemented under a government program to support talent recruitment and with the backing of the Agency for Strategic Initiatives
What can you expect?
We offer a comprehensive support program that includes:
Intensive Russian
2 years of additional classes for confidence in everyday life and at work
Job Search Guidance and Support
Training covering up-to-date job search methods, keys to acing interviews, and essential Russian-language business communication skills
Migration Support and Guidance
Assistance with the preparation of required documents for acquiring migration status and consultations regarding immigration regulations
Skills Enhancement and Career Change Programs:
Additional training in the most sought-after fields within the Irkutsk region’s labor market
Energy
Information Technology
Natural Resources Development
Construction
Summer schools hosted by top employers in the Irkutsk Region
Put your skills to work and gain the opportunity to become part of the team of leading companies in the Irkutsk region.
Dive into the world of real-life production and acquire practical insights from real professionals.
Why the Irkutsk Region?
The Irkutsk Region boasts a thriving economy, a vibrant culture, and breathtaking natural beauty. Here you’ll discover opportunities for career advancement, personal fulfillment, and exciting recreational pursuits. Lake Baikal, the jewel of Siberia, will inspire and energize your success!
