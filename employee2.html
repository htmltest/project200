Полномочия / обязанности
Руководство образовательной, финансовой, административной, хозяйственной и творческой деятельностью ИРНИТУ, формирование цели и задачи развития вуза;

Education

Стажировки

Overseas internship at Technopark Ulsan, South Korea

International internship program "Creation and development of innovative companies (SMARTiQ)”, in the business incubator “US Market access center”, San Jose, California, USA

Training under the program, Moscow School of Management Skolkovo

Intersectoral regional center for advanced training and retraining of personnel of the Federal State Budgetary Educational Institution of Higher Professional Education "ISTU"

Work experience

Since 2016 — acting Rector;
Since 2017 — Rector, Irkutsk National Research Technical University
2016
INRTU took the 83rd place among Russian universities in the International ranking of higher educational institutions ARES-2016 (Academic Ranking of World Universities - European Standard), held by the European Scientific and Industrial Chamber.
2016
IRNITU took 29th place in the National ranking of universities of the Russian Federation, which shows their demand from the Russian economy. The rating was carried out by the project "Social Navigator" MIA "Russia Today".
2016
The first flight of the new passenger liner MS-21-300. By order of the Irkutsk Aircraft Plant, IRNITU performed research, development and technological work in several areas, including machining, shaping and assembly operations. http://www.istu.edu/news/29457/

Work experience

2010 - 2016 — Vice-Rector for Innovation, National Research Irkutsk State Technical University
2014
According to the results of a comprehensive analysis of the competitiveness of ISTU, conducted by the Expert RA rating agency, the university has the best indicator among Russian universities according to the criterion "Innovation infrastructure" (ISTU score-3.59, with an average value for universities participating in the rating 2.5).
2016
IRNITU took the sixth place in monitoring the effectiveness of innovative activities of Russian universities. The analysis of the innovative activities of universities was carried out by the research group of the Russian Venture Company and ITMO University.
2016
IRNITU became one of the best accelerators and business incubators in Russia in 2016, taking 2nd place. The rating of the best accelerators and business incubators in Russia related to universities was presented by the analytical company UBI Global with the support of RVC and the Higher School of Economics.
