Registration and Visa support

No. 109-ФЗ “On Migration Registration of Foreign Citizens and Stateless Persons in the Russian Federation” dated July 18, 2006; ed. No. 18 dated 12/28/2013;

No. 110-ФЗ "On Amendments to the Federal Law" On the Legal Status of Foreign Citizens in the Russian Federation "dated July 18, 2006; edition No. 4 of December 28, 2013;

No. 9-Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation “On the Procedure for the Migration Registration of Foreign Citizens and Stateless Persons in the Russian Federation” dated January 15, 2007; as amended No. 9 dated 05/29/2014;

Article 18 of the Federal Law of December 30, 2001 No. 195-ФЗ “The Code of the Russian Federation on Administrative Offenses”; ed. No. 262 dated November 24, 2014;

  1. A foreign citizen who has arrived to study (work) at IRNITU / is going on vacation / going home for various reasons during the school year and crosses the border of the Russian Federation must provide his passport and migration card to the International Services Center (I 203) for 24 hours for the purpose of migration registration in the city of Irkutsk and receive within seven days a notification form on arrival (registration). When a foreign citizen changes his place of residence / stay in the Russian Federation (being on treatment in medical institutions, hotels, private addresses, travel around Russia / in the Irkutsk Region: Listvyanka, Olkhon Island, etc.) he/she should hand the same documents in to the International Services Center within one day and receive the notification form too.
  2. Violation of the registration rules for migration registration entails a fine of 40,000 to 500,000 rubles and expulsion from the Russian Federation.
  3. To extend the stay for students from the Community of Independent States countries, you must contact the Center for International Services 30 days before the end of the stay (registration).
  4. Visa extension: 45 days before the end of the visa period, you must submit the documents (passport, migration card, receipt of payment of the state fee, 1 matte photo, copy of the training contract / copy of the Ministry certificate). In case of violation of the deadline for ordering a new visa, a foreign student must go to his homeland. A foreign citizen will only be able to call back for training upon a new invitation from IRNITU.
  5. A foreign student must immediately inform the internal affairs bodies (police) about the loss of a foreign passport at the following address: St. Lermontov 102-A. Also, in the CMS (room И 203). The loss of a migration card, registration coupon must be reported to the Center for International Services (И 203).
  6. When traveling abroad / to other cities of Russia, a foreign student should do the following actions at the Center for International Services:
    • Notify the staff of the Center for International Services on his departure. Inform about the purpose of the trip, dates and place of stay (4 days before the departure).
    • Hand over the bypass sheet.
  7. A foreign student must inform the staff of the Center for International Services about receiving the following documents within 7 days from the date of their issue:
    • a temporary residence permit;
    • a residence permit;
    • a passport of the Russian Federation;
    • a new passport (for CIS countries).
  8. After university graduation or expulsion, a foreign student must leave Russia within the period established by the university administration (10 days after the order is issued). If a multiple-entry visa is valid at the time of expulsion, a foreign citizen must apply to the CMS officer (room И 203) for a transit visa.
  9. A foreign citizen must purchase a medical insurance before or after entering the territory of the Russian Federation. The presence of the insurance will provide you with inpatient and outpatient treatment. Medical insurance can be purchased at the CMS (room И 203).
